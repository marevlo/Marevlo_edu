Bayesian HPO. The GP optimizes the optimizer.

Every model in this track has hyperparameters — learning rate, dropout, number of layers, batch size. We've picked them by intuition, rules of thumb, and tables from earlier modules. Bayesian Hyperparameter Optimization (HPO) replaces intuition with a principled search: a GP surrogate models the relationship between hyperparameters and validation performance, then an acquisition function uses the GP's uncertainty to decide what to try next. In 20-50 evaluations, it finds configurations that grid search would need thousands of trials to reach.
